Comfort Controls

These settings determine the comfortable room temperature range VentIQ uses for recommendations.

Adjust the warm and cool thresholds to define what “comfortable” means for your room. VentIQ uses this band to decide when opening or closing a window can help regulate the space.

If the room is predicted to rise above this, VentIQ may suggest opening a window to cool things down.
If the room is predicted to fall below this, VentIQ may suggest closing windows or keeping warmer air in.

A tighter comfort range creates more frequent alerts. A wider range prioritizes fewer interventions and steadier comfort.

Sound Legend

What each sound means and how to interpret the alerts.

🔔
High beep — 880 Hz

Window action cue. This means VentIQ recommends regulating temperature by opening a window for airflow or cooling.

🌬️
Low beep — 660 Hz

Air quality cue. This means the room is getting stuffy and needs fresh air attention.
